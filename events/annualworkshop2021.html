Annual Photography Workshop 2021

Every year Click Club NITRR organizes a two day photography workshop, for past three years it's been organised virtually due to Covid-19 pandemic. It was organised on 13th and 14th Feb 2021.

Registration Deadline : 12th Feb 21, 11:59 PM IST

Free Registration

Individual Participation

Platform : Google Meet

Details


The 2 days Annual Photography Workshop by click, which was conducted in online mode for the year 2020 , was held on 13th and 14th February wherein knowledge about topics like basic camera handling, rules and composition and photo editing were imparted. It included a session from the guest speaker Rahul Ghanekar on DSLR and mobile Photography.
